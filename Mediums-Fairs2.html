    According to artprice, painting represented 61.2% of the global art auction turnover (sold at auction) in the first half of 2015. At the top end of the auction market, this distribution was even greater. The prominence of painting differed in the primary market, however: of the 49 fairs covered by Artsy which included painting, the medium accounted for only 30% of all works shown.

    In a number of Asian art fairs—where the tradition of painting, especially ink and wash painting, has an established history—painting was well-represented. KIAF, Art Taipei, Art Stage Singapore, and Art021 Shanghai Contemporary all appear within the top 10 fairs for painting.
